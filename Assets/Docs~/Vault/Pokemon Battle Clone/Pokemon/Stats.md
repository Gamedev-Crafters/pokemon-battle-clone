### Formulas
$$
PS =
\left\lfloor
\frac
	{( 2 \times Base + IV + \left\lfloor\frac{EV}{4}\right\rfloor \times Level)}
	{100}
\right\rfloor
+ Level + 10
$$
$$
OtherStat =
\left\lfloor
	\left(\left\lfloor
		\frac
			{(2 \times Base + IV + \left\lfloor\frac{EV}{4}\right\rfloor) \times Level}
			{100}
	\right\rfloor
	+ 5 \right) \times Nature
\right\rfloor
$$
